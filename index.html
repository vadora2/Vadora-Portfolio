My Journey

Learning through making.

Hi, I’m Vadora — I make ideas interactive.

I love designing with technology, but I always start with people — how they move, feel, click, get confused, and eventually understand something. My work usually sits somewhere between human-centred design, playful interaction, spatial thinking, and creative coding.

I’m into projects that feel useful but still have personality: websites, games, AI-powered systems, 3D spaces, event visuals, and small digital moments that make an experience feel more intuitive and alive.
